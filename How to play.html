How to play

To play this game you first have to log in with your friend. You have to see that its your chance to write or guess the word, If its your chance to write you have to write any word in the text input( that will be the word your friend will be guessing), then send it. Now its the chance of your friend, he/she will get an incomplete word (Ex. word is coding so your friend will get c_d_n_) then if he/she will guess it right he/she will score a point. If it is your chance then you have to guess the word, if you get it right you will also score a point.
